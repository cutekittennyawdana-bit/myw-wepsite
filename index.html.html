Intro

Everyone thrives to be a better person in life, and so many things can be done to find your true self and worth. with the "identified journey" you can find the deep person within you, with canon being alongside you in this journey, check out our awesome work. featuring a journey to inspect the beauty of jordan.

Our magazine features the three pretiest cities in jordan, madaba, the city of mosaics and art, where art and creativity gather in one place to make one of most prettiest pieces. with amman the city of connection and communication, where people talk, chat and socalize. and aqaba! the wonderful city aqaba that has so many things to see and interact with.

our Work

Our work has so many things to see and know about, with so many inspirations and ideas to awaken your true self. with of course, canon helping you all the way there. with the exact locations! so you can go inspect them yourselves and be impressed.

Also, we feature canons most compatible camera to go on with your journey. with how sustainable it is, with saving battery and storage, you can capture the moment anywhere you go. these pictures effect deeply in the youngsters heart. which makes them develop a new personality and mindset. where they can look into the world in another way.

About us

we make alot of other media for canon. because canon is a great company for making our youngsters lives better by just providing the best cameras out there that lets them freely and creatively control the settings of their own pictures. visit canons official site for more info.
